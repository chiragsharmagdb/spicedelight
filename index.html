Our Story

Welcome to Spice Delight

Founded in 2005, Spice Delight has been serving authentic Indian cuisine with a modern twist. Our journey began in a small kitchen in Mumbai, where our founder, Chef Raj Patel, dreamed of sharing the vibrant flavors of India with the world.

Today, we continue that tradition with carefully sourced ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and a passion for creating memorable dining experiences.

Every dish tells a story - from the street foods of Delhi to the royal kitchens of Rajasthan, we bring India's diverse culinary heritage to your table.
